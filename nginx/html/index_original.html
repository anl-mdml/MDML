Manufacturing Data & Machine Learning Layer

The Manufacturing Data & Machine Learning Layer (MDML for short) was created under Argonne National Laboratory's Manufacturing Science and Engineering Initiative. The MDML's purpose is to provide lab spaces for the Manufacturing Initiative with easy ways to perform analytics, create dashboards, connect to HPC resources, and more. This can be done in real-time as well as in batch for large training jobs on ALCF resources. This page serves as the home page for any information pertaining to the MDML.
